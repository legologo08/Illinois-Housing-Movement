About Us

Expanding Affordable Housing in Illinois is a grassroots movement dedicated to creating more affordable housing options for everyone in Illinois. We believe in a future where all families have a safe, affordable place to call home.

The Issue

In Illinois, there is a shortage of over 300,000 affordable rental homes for low-income residents. Rising real estate costs, limited funding, and restrictive zoning laws make it challenging for low- and middle-income families to find housing they can afford.

  • Over 30% of Illinois families spend more than they can afford on housing.
  • Affordable housing is crucial for healthier, safer communities.

Our Goals

  • Advocate for increased funding for affordable housing projects.
  • Encourage zoning law reforms to allow for higher-density developments.
  • Work with nonprofits and local governments to expand housing options.
